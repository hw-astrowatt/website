3rd Niparo Space
Sustainability Summit the 3rd Niparo Summit on Space Sustainability in May, introducing the work AstroWatt is doing to the audience.

Attendees from the UKSA, ESA, academia and industry attended the Summit, hosted at Heriot-Watt, to improve understanding of the challenges and opportunities relating so sustainability of and insurance for In-Orbit Servicing, Assembly and Manufacturing techniques, broader space activities, and financial space innovation. The previous two summits have been successful, with resulting work influencing governments and academic research.
