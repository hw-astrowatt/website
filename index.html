taking Heriot-Watt to space

who we are

AstroWatt is a coalition of space-focused student engineering teams at Heriot-Watt University. In 2025, the founding societies Heriot-Watt CanSat Team, Heriot-Watt Rocketry Society, Watts-Up, and Heriot-Satt realised that there was an unprecendented opportunity for knowledge and resource sharing across their respective societies, and so AstroWatt was born. By sharing PCB designs, software, and engineering resources, every member society gets to avoid reinventing the wheel and focusing on those elements of their mission that truly matters.

Today:

  • Heriot-Watt CanSat Team is preparing for their fourth entry into the AAS CanSat Competition this June

  • Heriot-Watt Rocketry Society is staging viewings of historic rocket launches and preparing to compete in student rocketry competitions

  • Watts-Up are planning a series of weather balloon launches to test flight electronics and photograph the curvature of the earth

  • Heriot-Satt are working on a top-secret project...
